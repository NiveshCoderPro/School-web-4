SSVM world school

Shree Sarasswathi Vidhyaah Mandheer (SSVM)was established in 1998 and is a group of co-educational CBSE Institutions located at Mettupalayam, Coimbatore, Tamilnadu,India ,founded by the visionary Managing Trustee and Correspondent, Dr Manimekalai Mohan.The school is affiliated to CBSE Board.The School is managed by Sri Srisha Educational and Charitable Trust.SSVM Institutions match International Standards in academic learning and extra-curricular activities. The campuses have technologically superior and serene milieu, supported by globally proven teaching-learning methods, where knowledge, intellect and diversity come together to inspire young minds towards a holistic development. SSVM creates a class of promising leaders and social change-makers for a better tomorrow.

Culture of SSVM

To celebrate and mark the significance of Indo-Russian relations, A Cultural Programme was organized. Dancers from Russia performed with a lot of zeal and vigour and enthralled the audience. Amazing and unknown facts about different dances and their culture were revealed by the Russian dancers. To bring a meaningful end to the programme, traditional dance was performed by our students. This programme was step-in strengthening Indo-Russian relation with cultural and educational exchanges.
